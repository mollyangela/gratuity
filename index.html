What Factors Influence the Amount of Gratuity in Dubai?

Understanding the factors that influence the amount of gratuity in Dubai is essential for employees navigating their end-of-service benefits. Gratuity, a form of compensation provided to employees upon termination of service, can vary significantly based on several key elements. This article will explore these factors in detail, helping you gain a comprehensive understanding of how gratuityin dubai is calculated .

How to Calculate Gratuity in UAE?

Key Factors Influencing Gratuity Amount

Several factors play a crucial role in determining the amount of gratuity an employee receives in Dubai. Here are the most significant ones:

Basic Salary

The basic salary is the primary factor in gratuity calculations. Only the basic salary is considered, excluding allowances, bonuses, or other benefits.

Calculation Impact

A higher basic salary directly increases the gratuity amount. For instance, if an employee has a basic salary of AED 10,000, their gratuity will be significantly higher than that of an employee earning AED 5,000.

Duration of Service

The length of time an employee has worked for the same employer also affects gratuity calculations.

Service Years

Employees must complete at least one year of continuous service to be eligible for gratuity. The longer the duration of service, the higher the gratuity amount.

Calculation Method

The gratuity is calculated based on the number of years worked: - For the first five years: 21 days of salary for each year. - For each additional year beyond five: 30 days of salary for each year.
